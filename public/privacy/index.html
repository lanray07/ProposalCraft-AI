Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

ProposalCraft AI helps users create service business proposals from the project details they choose to provide. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

Information You Provide

The app may process proposal details such as business name, contact name, client name, service type, project description, pricing, deposit, timeline, and optional business contact information. This information is used only to generate proposal text and PDF files requested by the user.

Local Storage

The browser widget may save form inputs locally on your device so repeat proposals are faster to prepare. This local data stays in your browser unless you clear it or reset the form.

Server Processing

Proposal generation requests are processed by the ProposalCraft AI MCP server hosted on Vercel. The server returns generated proposal content and does not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

ProposalCraft AI is hosted on Vercel and is used through ChatGPT/OpenAI app surfaces. Those services may process technical request information according to their own policies.

Data Retention

ProposalCraft AI does not provide user accounts and does not maintain a customer database for generated proposals. Hosting providers may retain operational logs for security, debugging, and reliability.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact banksmi@mail.com.
