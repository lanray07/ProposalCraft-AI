ProposalCraft AI

Create structured service proposals and PDFs.

ProposalCraft AI helps service businesses turn job details into client-ready proposals with clear scope, pricing, payment terms, assumptions, approval copy, and polished PDF exports.

What it does

  • Generates proposals for trades and service businesses
  • Uses deterministic templates by service type
  • Creates branded PDF proposal exports
  • Supports regenerate and explain actions

Supported use cases

  • Landscaping, paving, cleaning, plumbing, HVAC, roofing
  • General contractor, remodeling, flooring, electrical
  • Pressure washing, handyman, moving, appliance repair
  • And other service proposal workflows
